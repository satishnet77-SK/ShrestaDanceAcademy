Our Story

A Sanctuary of Classical Arts

Shresta Dance Academy was founded with a singular mission — to preserve, nurture, and celebrate the glorious heritage of India's classical dance traditions in the heart of Hyderabad.

Our guiding philosophy is rooted in the belief that dance is not merely movement, but a sacred language — a dialogue between the body, the soul, and the divine.

Every student who walks through our doors embarks on a transformative journey that builds discipline, grace, confidence, and a deep connection to cultural roots.

Our Disciplines

Classical Dance Forms

🌸
Bharatanatyam

One of India's oldest classical dance forms from Tamil Nadu, known for its precise footwork, expressive hand gestures, and devotional themes.

🦚
Kuchipudi

A vibrant dance-drama form from Andhra Pradesh, combining graceful movements, expressive abhinaya, and lyrical storytelling.

🌺
Odissi

A lyrical classical dance from Odisha, distinguished by its sculptural poses, fluid movements, and deep spiritual connection.

✨
Classical Fusion

A curated blend of classical forms that celebrates the shared vocabulary of Indian dance — rhythm, expression, and devotion.
